# Privacy Policy — ESEA Team Stats

**Last updated:** April 17, 2026

## Data Collection

ESEA Team Stats does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or account systems.

## API Usage

The extension makes requests to the [FACEIT Data API](https://open.faceit.com) to retrieve publicly available match and player statistics. These requests are made directly from your browser to FACEIT's servers. No data from these requests is sent to any third party.

## Permissions

- **Host permission (`https://open.faceit.com/*`):** Required to fetch match details, player history, and per-player statistics from the FACEIT public API.
- **Content script (`www.faceit.com`):** Required to display aggregated stats on FACEIT match pages.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/evanvnguyen/faceit-esea-team-stats](https://github.com/evanvnguyen/faceit-esea-team-stats/issues).
